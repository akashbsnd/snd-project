Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book online at supremenomads.com/booking or call us at (704) 561-1927. We'll confirm your appointment within 24 hours.

Do I need to bring my car anywhere?

No. Supreme Nomads Detailing is a fully mobile service — we come to your home, workplace, or any preferred location in the Charlotte area.

How long does a detail take?

An Express detail takes approximately 1–2 hours. A Premium detail takes 3–5 hours depending on the vehicle's condition and size.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Charlotte, Concord, Cornelius, Harrisburg, Huntersville, Indian Trail, Matthews, Mooresville, Waxhaw, Lake Norman, and Pineville in NC, plus Fort Mill, Indian Land, Lake Wylie, and Rock Hill in SC.

How long does ceramic coating last?

Our ceramic coating application provides 5–7 years of protection with proper maintenance. It includes a 2-step paint correction prior to application.

Do you offer maintenance plans?

Yes — maintenance wash plans are available to customers who purchase a paint protection package. Plans include 6 or 12 washes scheduled within 40-day intervals.
